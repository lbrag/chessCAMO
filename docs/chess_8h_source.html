10 /*
11 Project Description:
12 chessCAMO is a newly proposed chess variant that stands for "Calculations Always Make Opportunities"
13 and was inspired by the very popular chess variant "Crazyhouse" (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crazyhouse).
14 The "CAMO" portion comes from the idea that in this variant, at any turn, a player can sacrifice making a
15 move to replace one of their pieces with a piece from the "piece reservoir". This has the affect of
16 camouflaging each piece that is currently on the board. The piece reservoir is limited in size and
17 cannot be re-stocked with pieces. Once a player decides to use a piece from the reservoir to replace
18 one of his existing pieces on the board, their turn is over. An obvious rule is that player cannot use
19 the reservoir while in check as they must make a move to avoid check. Additionally, just like in the
20 Crazyhouse variant, a player cannot directly promote a pawn from the reservoir. That is a pawn from
21 the reservoir must be placed on the board first and is only allowed to be promoted on the next move.
22

28  4) GUI - nice to be able to move pieces with mouse rather than inputting coordinates.
29 
30  ChessCAMO:

32  2) Allow players to replace existing pieces with reservoir pieces
33  3) Implement abovementioned check and pawn promotion rules
